ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & MACHINE LEARNING
Course Objectives

To gain foundational proficiency in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) techniques.

To understand heuristic and probabilistic approaches to intelligent systems.

To build predictive and classification models using decision trees, neural networks, and clustering methods.

To grasp essential tools and concepts of fuzzy logic and probabilistic reasoning, training, and evaluation.

Course Outcomes

After successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

Formulate problems suitable for AI and ML solutions.

Understand how AI agents make decisions and solve problems optimally under constraints.

Represent knowledge formally and reason logically or probabilistically.

Understand classical supervised learning models and performance evaluation techniques.

Gain a broad understanding of advanced ML and AI paradigms, including neural, fuzzy, and unsupervised models.
